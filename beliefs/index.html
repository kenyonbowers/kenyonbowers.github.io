Beliefs


The Bible

I believe the King James Bible (as found in the 1900 Pure Cambridge Edition) is the only perfect and infallible Word of God in the English language. I believe Bomberg's 1524-26 Mikraot Gedolot 2nd Edition of the Masoretic Text and Scrivener's 1894 Textus Receptus (with the "Amen" amended in Ephesians 6:24) are the correct texts from which to translate the Bible.

(Psalms 12:6-7, Psalm 119:89, 160, Psalm 138:2, Isaiah 40:8, Matthew 5:18, John 10:35)


The Trinity

I believe that God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit/Ghost are One. I am a Classical Trinitarian.

(Micah 5:2, Matthew 3:13-17, John 1:1-3, 14-18, John 10:30, Hebrews 1:1-12, 1 John 5:7)


Free Grace

I believe salvation is, and always has been, by grace through faith. Works cannot save you—we cannot be "good enough" to get to Heaven. We need Jesus' blood applied to our account. We do not need to repent of our sins (change our life) to be saved.

(John 3:16-17, 36, John 5:24, John 6:47, Romans 3:24, Romans 3:24, Romans 4:1-8, 14-16, 24-25, Ephesians 2:8-9, Titus 3:4-7)


Eternal Security

I believe that once a person is saved, they are forever sealed in Jesus' hand. No one, including themselves, can cause them to lose their salvation. While Christ's will for believers is to be holy, believers still have the free will to leave the faith and/or live wicked lives. This, however, has no effect on their salvation nor does it prove they were "never truly saved." They will be judged temporally and will not receive rewards at the judgment seat of Christ, but their salvation is not dependent on how they live.

(Ecclesiastes 3:14, John 3:36, John 5:24, John 6:47, John 10:28-29, 1 Corinthians 3:15, Ephesians 1:13, Ephesians 4:30)


Hell & Eternal Punishment

I believe Hell is a real place of judgment where the lost go when they die. Because of our sin, we are at enmity with God and we can't pay our sin debt on our own. As a result, we will be seperated eternally from His love in Hell—and later the Lake of Fire prepared for Satan and his angels—if we don't get saved.

(Proverbs 27:20, Isaiah 14:15, Matthew 23:33, Matthew 25:41-46, Mark 9:43-47, Luke 12:5, Luke 16:22-26, Revelation 20:13-14, Revelation 21:8)


Pre-Tribulation Rapture

I believe that the rapture of the Church will be before the tribulation. The Church will not be present during the tribulation but people can still be saved during it.

(Luke 21:23, 36, 1 Corinthians 15:51-57, 1 Thessalonians 1:10, 1 Thessalonians 4:13-18, 1 Thessalonians 5:9, 2 Thessalonians 3:2-5, Revelation 4:1, 4, 10-11)


Local Church Autonomy

I believe in local, New Testament church autonomy. No organization is to be over the local church, and it should govern itself. The pastor has the responsibility as the overseer, or under shepherd, of the flock, known as the local church.

(Acts 9:31, Acts 13:1, Acts 14:23, Acts 15:41, Acts 16:5, Acts 20:28, Romans 16:4, 16, 1 Corinthians 11:16, 2 Corinthians 11:28, 1 Timothy 3:5, Revelation 1:11, 20)
