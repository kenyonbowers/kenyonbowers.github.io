Striving to Please the Master

Bringing the Scripture and the Gospel to the internet.

In late 2022, God pricked my heart about the lack of Biblical Truth on the internet. I was following my own path and what I wanted. I was busy programming the things that I wanted. But I am so glad God has made me to realize that He wants to use me in His ministry. He has allowed me to use my skills to outreach and evangelize.
