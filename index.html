About Kenyon

I, Kenyon, am an Independent Fundamentalist Baptist (IFB). I am committed to defending the King James Bible and the Traditional Texts of both the Old and New Testaments (Masoretic Text & Textus Receptus). My mission and goal is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with the world through online media. Likewise, I seek to teach and defend Bible truths through my online presence. My desire is to help believers grow in their faith and knowledge of God, equip them for effective ministry, and encourage them to live godly lives. I am committed to upholding the doctrines of the Christian faith, defending the truth against error, and promoting biblical separation from the world. Ultimately, my desire is to glorify God and see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel.
