    Beliefs

    • My Mission

    As an Independent Fundamental Baptist committed to the King James Bible, my mission is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with the world through online media. I seek to use the internet as a tool to reach people from all walks of life with the message of salvation, emphasizing the need for placing your faith in Christ alone. I believe that the King James Bible is the inspired, inerrant, and infallible Word of God (not double inspiration), and I seek to teach and defend its truths through this online ministry. My goal is to help believers grow in their faith and knowledge of God, equip them for effective ministry, and encourage them to live godly lives. I am committed to upholding the historic doctrines of the Christian faith, defending the truth against error, and promoting biblical separation from the world. Ultimately, my desire is to glorify God and see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel.
